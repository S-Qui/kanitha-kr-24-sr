Children's Ministry

About
The Children's Ministry

Teaching the Christian faith to the young ones. The Children's Ministry is composed of two pillars:

  • Church School
  • Brigade
Catering for both boys and girls.

'Let the children come to me.' Mark 10:14

Mission

We exist to provide holistic Christian instruction, training, and capacity building for all children and their mentors in order to accept Christ as their savior, equip them to understand their Christian faith and apply it in their lives.


Vision

To teach, and make visible to all children the Love of God and their holistic identity to Christ and their relationship to others.

Church School

The Boy's and Girl's Brigade

The Brigade is open to those who attend and are classified in this ranks:

  • 5-8 year's Explorers
  • 8-11 year's Junior Section
  • 11-14 year's Senior Section
  • 14-18 year's Brigadiers
