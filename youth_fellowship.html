Youth Fellowship

About

This fellowship seeks to embrace activities of young people in the Church, to draw youth into God’s Kingdom and train them as disciples, and to enlist their efforts in evangelization and fellowship in co-operation in the promotion of God’s kingdom among youth of P.C.E.A

Youth Motto: Love, Unity and Services.

Mission

  1. Bring young people in fellowship, discipleship, and evangelism. (To equip, uplift and up build young people)
  2. Raise and equip emerging young leaders to serve in the Church and society
  3. Empower young people physically, economically, socially and politically.


Vision

The youth department hopes to see a vibrant and well-coordinated youth movement in East Africa and beyond.


Core Values

  1. Bible and Christ-centered service
  2. Democratic participation and inclusiveness
  3. Teamwork and networking
  4. Discipline and integrity
