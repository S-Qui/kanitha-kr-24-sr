Department

JPRC endeavors to realize the set objectives for, on behalf and/or representing the church. The summary of the objectives of J.P.R.C are as provided in Act 26.12 of the church Practice and Procedure Manual as:

  1. To take appropriate actions to address all social, economic and political issues that are likely to affect the community the ministry of the Church at any given time and in any sphere.
  2. To Lead the church at all levels in undertaking functions including the establishment of partnerships and collaboration towards environmental protection and conservation.
  3. To plan and arrange seminars and workshop to create awareness among Church leaders and members on the Government policies to enhance their participation in their implementation.
  4. To promote and advocate for justice and harmonious coexistence within societies.
  5. To act as the conscience of the society and to endeavor to promote and protect the observance of human rights.
  6. To advocate for the creation and expansion of democratic space including the exercise and sustenance of democracy.
  7. To advise the Church through the Business Committee on issues about which the Church should make a declaration or state its position.
  8. To enter into partnership with other like-minded institutions for the aim of furtherance and/or execution of its purpose, objectives and budget.
