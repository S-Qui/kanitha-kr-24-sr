About Us

At P.C.E.A Kihumo Church: Rironi Parish, we believe in fostering a thoughtful, prayerful environment where all can deepen their understanding of scripture, growing in community, and serve with humanity. We invite you to join us for worship and discover the peace and purpose that come from walking together in faith.
