History

The History of P.C.E.A Kihumo Church: Rironi Parish
The 1st church was built here in Rironi between the home’s of Rev. George Nyoro and Rev. Rufus Waigi Nyoro in 1920-1923. Unfortunately it was burnt down in 1928 by …. during the …… because of a hatred harbored towards Christianity.

The 2nd church was built here where P.C.E.A Kihumo Church stands. The 3rd and 4th attempt to rebuild the church were demolished by people who didn’t want anything to do with Christianity. The 5th church was also demolished due to an emergency and the church's things were stored by Rev. George Nyoro. The stored wooden poles and metal sheets were used to build the 6th church where Rironi Secondary School is located today. From that point everything was moved and used to start P.C.E.A Rironi Mother Church on the land that Rev. George Nyoro donated making it the 7th church.

P.C.E.A Kihumo Church is the 8th ….

In 2001, Rev. Edmard Gichini Nyoro and his wife Emily Wanjiku Gichini and their children donated this land at the Ndeiya Kirk Session. The session was lead by Rev. Henry Gacheru Kaira. Thanks and appreciation was given to the family. The land was to be prepared and prayed for by those who would come from P.C.E.A Rironi Church now making it the Mother Church.
