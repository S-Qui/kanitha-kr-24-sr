P.C.E.A Kihumo Church

A TIMELINE OF THE HISTORY OF THE CHURCH

1920-1923

Kanitha wa Mbere ituuraini riri riothe ria Rironi wakitwo gatagati ka nyumba ya mutigairi George Nyoro na kwa mutigairi Rufus Waigi Nyoro 1920-1923, ni wacinirwo ni mumo 1928 hindi ya muthirigu ni undu wa guthura uhoro wa ukiristiano.

1924-1928

Kanitha wa keri wakitwo haha twakite kanitha uyu wa P.C.E.A Kihumo Church. Kanitha wa gatatu na wa kana niwatharirio ni andu aria matendaga uhoro wa ukiristiano.

1929-2000

Kanitha wa gatano wakwo onaguo Ndwahuthiriruo niundu wa emergency, Niwatharirio na indo ikiigwo ni Mutigairi George Nyoro, mihirigo iyo na mabati niyo yathire kwambiriria kanitha wa Rironi haria hakitwo Rironi Secondary school na niguo wa gatandatu. Kuuma hau niguo wathamirio na indo icio nicio ciathire kwanjia Kanitha wa PCEA Rironi Mother church kieya-ini kiria kiaheanirwo ni Mutigairi George Nyoro na niguo wa mugwanja.

2001

Kuri Mwaka wa Ngiri igiri na imwe (2001) niguo Mutigairi Edmard Gichini Nyoro na mwendwa-niiri Emily Wanjiku Gichini hamwe na ciana ciao niguo maheaniri plot ino kuri Ndeiya Kirk Session itongoretio ni Rev Henry Gacheru Kaira. Kiama nigiakenire na gigicokia ngatho kuri Family lyo. Kiama gikiuga hahariririo niguo hahoyagirwo niaria makuhiriirie itura riri moimite PCEA Rironi Church iria riu niyo Mother Church.

2002

14/07/2002 ~ niguo kiama kiamurire kieya giki gia PCEA Kihumo church na niguo Parish yothe yarehire members a kihumo aria mari igana rimwe andu agima ohamwe na ciana.
21/07/2002 ~ nigwo wari muthenya wa mbere guthomera haha kieyaini giki. Kanitha wambere wari gitayaya kiria giakitwo na itingi ikaigirirwo mabati ikumi (10) na makunia miena, na Vestry yari oya makunia.
18/11/2002 ~ nigwo twenjire mutaaro wa muthingi wa Church Hall ya Guthomera na a member makiirutira na wendo munene muno. Hau Kanitha wamakunia wari niho kigongona gia kanitha uyu turi kiri nayo Vestry haria yari noho iri riu.

2014

Kwenja muthingi wa kanitha ino wari 06/07/2014 uria watongoririo ni Mutigairi Rev. Geofrey Watute uria wari Parish Minister Rironi Church

2015-2017

Kuuma hau 23/03/2015 tukiigiria ihiga rira riatongoretio ni Rev Alice Watheri Kimani (Moderator Limuru Presbytery)
2016-2017 kanitha ukigitwo.

Twagita kanitha, tukirugama tondu niwonekire nduri na riingiriro Tukiaria na Mwanake wa Late Eldmard Gichini Nyoro, Mr Edison Murimi Gichini agiitikira gutwenderia Plot ingi ya muigana wa 50x100 mwena wa Muhuro wa Kanitha.Committee iria yaheirwo wira wa kuhariria Harambee iyo ya kugura plot iyo yatongoretio ni James Mburu Njenga.

2019

Tugicoka mwakoini tukiruta wira wothe wa cuuma.

2020

Tukiruta wira wa finishing ya plasta.Mwakaini ucio noguo twaingirire kanitha uyu niundu wa Covid 19 na church hall nitwanyitanite muno natutingiahotire kugia na social distancing iria yendekanaga niundu wa kugiririria kugwatania murimu wa Covid 19.
